Gameplay Loop

How It Works

Simple idle mechanics, deep strategy. No real-time combat.

01
🎰
Gacha
Roll to collect Slime NFTs across 5 rarity tiers. Each with unique weapons and powers.
02
⚔️
Dungeon Run
Send your Slime in, choose difficulty, wait for timer, collect loot automatically.
03
📦
Loot & Boxes
Collect potions, ores, equipment. Hard dungeons drop $SLIME, Bankr and ETH boxes.
04
⚗️
Forge
Combine dungeon materials to craft powerful potions and equipment for your Slimes.
05
⬆️
Upgrade
Use materials and $SLIME to level up NFTs. Higher level Slimes clear harder dungeons.
